Cali AI: A Calamansi Disease Detector System

It is a smart and easy-to-use system designed to help farmers take care of their calamansi plants. Using advanced image analysis and intelligent algorithms, it can quickly identify and classify common plant diseases. With Cali AI, farmers can spot problems early, reduce crop losses, and keep their calamansi plants healthy. It's like having a helpful assistant in the field, guiding you to take timely actions for a better harvest
